City Rustic Designs

in this project, I was responsible for making the logo, colot palette, and typography.

Project Overview:

Re-lunching the company again after stopping for 2 years.

Brand Slang:

Where artwork meets woodwork

Brand Identity:

City Rustic Designs is a brand of high-quality charcuterie boards, cutting boards and butcher blocks. Each piece is 100% uniquely handcrafted in Canada with a modern design and authenticity. With many years of experience using sustainable, natural, and food-safe materials, an excellent quality product is our#1 goal.

Project goals:

Make a visual identity that represents City Rustic Designs for 2023 and beyond. This includes a new logo and Brand style guidelines (typography, colors, and usage). After a 2-year break from business and woodworking, our goal is to re-introduce City Rustic Designs with a new look and strategy.

Product:

Cutting boards, butcher blocks, and charcuterie boards.

Service:

To provide our customers with the best all-natural and unique products at an affordable price.

Target Audience:

Ages:

25-60 years old.

Gender:

Women more than men.

Characteristics:

People who like to cook their meals, party hosts, working singles, working families, chefs, and food enthusiasts.

What should be included/excluded when addressing this audience?

Healthy foodies, durable, sustainable, easy to handle, assured safety, customization, all natural.

The most effective tone and imagery when communicating with Audience:

  • Bringing the beauty of nature into your home.
  • One-of-a-kind heirlooms quality 100% handcrafted.
  • All-natural finish.
  • Made in the Canada.
  • Minimal waste, sustainable.

Concepts:

Wood grain, circles or lines, name or only the C.

Design Elements:

  • Digital format of the logo (legible) to be branded on wood, and to be printed on marketing materials.
  • Photography session.
