Coffee Card Design Competition

The Brief

In this project, the contest was to design a loyalty card for the Campus store at the Yukon University. The design needs to be a creative concept and different from the University brand guidelines.

The Research

Since the store is located at the campus, the targeted audience are the current student. Coffee can provide a lot of benefits to students. Coffee is known for its ability to fight fatigue and increase energy levels.

The Concept

The key words that stand out of the research were: increase and energy. I searched synonyms for those words and found Fuel and rocket were among the alternatives! The concept was born from these synonyms. Fuel represent energy and rocket represent the increase.

I did several sketches and set on the choosen one. Yellow is the color of the sun and energy. I matched with light blue to represent the sky and clear mind!

For the fonts, I combined Capitol (medium) with Cormorant Garamond (light) to provide futuristic character to the design.

The Results

On the right side, you can see the end design that I created. As for the contest, I came second in line for the wining.
