Magazine Cover Designs

The Brief

I was asked to design different concepts (design mock-ups) for a magazine cover.

we have been asked to photograph and design the layout of different versions of ‘Multimedia Magazine’. The covers feature portrait photography demonstrating basic, 1 light, lighting techniques. Each cover features a different portrait headshot demonstrating a different lighting technique (using only 1 light). This assignment was designed to showcase our understanding of basic retouching while demonstrating our skills in using the type tools and preparing a document for printing.
