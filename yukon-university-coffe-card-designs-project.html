Coffee Card Design Competition

The Brief

In this project, the contest was to design a loyalty card for the Campus store at the Yukon University. The design needs to follow the University's brand guidelines.

The loyalty card has to include the logo, six stamp spot plus the free drink!

Design Elements

The design elements were provided by the marketing and branding team as PNG. Color palette and fonts specifications were provided as well.

*NOTE: the logo and design elements are the property of Yukon University and may not be used for any other project or saved to personal devices.

The Concept

I decided to create a pattern using the floral elements that are unique to the Yukon environment, which I nedded to transferre all design elements into vector art.

I felt that would be the best way to reflect that uniqueness in the coffee loyalty card

The Results

On the right side, you can see the end design that I created. As for the contest, I won the Competition!
